Origin Story

It all started in New York in the year 1935. Vito Scaletta's great grandparents just immigrated to America. When they immigrated they encountered all different kinds of people and decided to include the community in their traditional Sunday family meals. Not long after that they opened up a restaurant to spread their cooking to the city, and on and on it went! Now there are locations all over the nation to share and pass on the Scaletta tradition, reachings millions of families!
